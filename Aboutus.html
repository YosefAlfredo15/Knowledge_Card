About Us

Alkhaw Restourant is a privately formed private company. Established on January 15, 2020 located at Mulyosari Street No. 20 Surabaya, which was later designated as Alkhaw Restaurant as well as its head office and administration. At first this restaurant was only a restaurant named " Buffet Alkhaw".

Alkhaw Restaurant is engaged in managing specific food that comes from the italian state, where the reason for establishing this restaurant is the result of a survey that the lack of restaurant facilities, especially those that provide specific Italian food in the city of Surabaya. By seeing this opportunity, alkhaw restaurant was established in 2020. This field of business continues to grow to this day, where there are several similar businesses managed by other parties..
